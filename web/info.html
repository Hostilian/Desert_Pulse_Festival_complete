Venues and addresses

Solar Stage

Main open-air stage for larger concert slots.

North Field, Solara Desert Park, Arizona, USA

Capacity: 20,000

Mirage Tent

Covered venue for electronic and late-evening performances.

East Dunes, Solara Desert Park, Arizona, USA

Capacity: 8,000

Dune Arena

Outdoor arena focused on indie, alternative, and hip-hop sets.

South Ridge, Solara Desert Park, Arizona, USA

Capacity: 12,000

Oasis Workshop Dome

Compact space for talks, workshops, and smaller sessions.

Central Oasis, Solara Desert Park, Arizona, USA

Capacity: 1,500

Accommodation

  • Festival camping next to the Central Oasis.
  • Partner hotels in nearby Solara town.
  • Quiet zone for visitors who prefer lower night noise.

Parking and transport

Parking opens each day at 08:00. Shuttle buses run between parking and the main gate.

Visitor rules

Bring water, sunscreen, and your ticket (digital or printed). Follow staff instructions in all venue zones.
