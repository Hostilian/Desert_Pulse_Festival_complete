Venue and address

Solar Stage

Main open-air stage for headliners and large concerts.

North Field, Solara Desert Park, Arizona, USA

Capacity: 20000

Mirage Tent

Covered tent focused on electronic, experimental, and late-night sets.

East Dunes, Solara Desert Park, Arizona, USA

Capacity: 8000

Dune Arena

Outdoor arena for indie, hip-hop, and alternative shows.

South Ridge, Solara Desert Park, Arizona, USA

Capacity: 12000

Oasis Workshop Dome

Small venue for talks, workshops, acoustic sessions, and artist meetings.

Central Oasis, Solara Desert Park, Arizona, USA

Capacity: 1500

Accommodation

  • Festival camping near the Central Oasis.
  • Partner hotels in Solara town.
  • Quiet zone for families and early sleepers.

Parking and transport

Parking opens daily at 08:00. Shuttle buses connect the parking area with the festival entrance.

Visitor rules

Bring water bottles, sunscreen, comfortable shoes and a printed or digital ticket.
