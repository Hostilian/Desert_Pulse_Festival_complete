Your festival day

  1. 08:00 · Parking opens at the south entrance.
  2. 09:30 · Shuttle buses start running between parking and the main gate.
  3. 10:00 · First programme block begins across all four venues.
  4. 13:00 · Workshop sessions at Oasis Workshop Dome.
  5. 20:00 · Headline concerts at Solar Stage and Dune Arena.
  6. 23:30 · Late-night sets continue at Mirage Tent.

Accommodation & transport

Camping

Festival camping

Next to the Central Oasis, with a separate quiet zone for visitors who prefer lower night noise.

Hotels

Partner hotels

Several hotels in nearby Solara town offer festival rates and shuttle pickup.

Transport

Shuttle & parking

Parking opens daily at 08:00. Shuttles run every 15 minutes from the lot to the main gate.

Rentals & extras

Gear

Equipment rental

Low-back festival chairs, compact shade canopies, and USB power banks are available to rent near Central Oasis. Reserve online with your ticket or pick up on site while stock lasts.

Lockers

Lockers & charging

Keyed lockers in three sizes and staffed phone-charging bays sit beside the main gate. Overnight locker access ends 30 minutes after the last Mirage Tent set.

Wellbeing

Medical & accessibility

A 24-hour medical tent sits west of Solar Stage. Step-free routes, accessible viewing platforms, and a dedicated assistance line are listed in the festival app and at guest services.

Visitor rules
